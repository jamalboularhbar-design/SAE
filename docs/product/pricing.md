# NexusAI — Unified Pricing Model

## Pricing Philosophy

Three products, one ladder. Every customer enters at the lowest friction point and upgrades naturally.

```
Free/OSS → Templates ($49-199) → Playbooks ($299-999/mo) → Enterprise (custom)
```
