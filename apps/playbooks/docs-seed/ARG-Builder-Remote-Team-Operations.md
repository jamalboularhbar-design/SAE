# ARG-Builder: Remote Team Operations

## Executive Summary

This playbook provides the operational framework, roles, and execution guidance for **Remote Team Operations** within the ARG-Builder business operating system. It is part of the ARG-Builder operational library for **People & Culture** teams building and scaling a B2B SaaS platform.

## Objectives

| Objective | Success Criteria | Owner |

| Standardize remote team operations practices | Documented process adopted by team | People & Culture lead |
| Reduce execution variance | < 10% deviation from playbook steps | Ops |
| Enable measurable outcomes | KPIs tracked in weekly review | Leadership |
