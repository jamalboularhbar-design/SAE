Luxury Moroccan travel concierge. Founded 2017. Marrakech-based, globally networked.

**Domain:** riadandroutes.com
**Workspace slug in the platform:** `riad-routes` (routing compatibility — do not change)
**Tagline:** *Luxury Moroccan experiences for HNW Americans — bespoke, discreet, end-to-end.*

**Voice characteristics (used in the agent persona inside the app):**
- Sophisticated & discreet — refined tone, privacy, exclusivity
- Knowledgeable — deep expertise in luxury travel, Morocco, accommodations, cultural nuances
- Proactive & detail-oriented — anticipates needs, meticulous itineraries, logistics
- Resourceful — exclusive access, complex arrangements, issue resolution
- Empathetic — understands the desires of discerning travelers
- Communication: formal yet personable, precise language, emphasizes exclusivity, utmost confidentiality

**The 7-stage Autopilot workflow** (this is the canonical R&R process — used as a demo when pitching the platform):
1. Inquiry & Qualification — receive initial inquiry, assess client profile (net worth, travel history, preferences), determine fit
2. Consultation & Proposal — detailed consultation, present bespoke itinerary highlighting unique experiences
3. Itinerary Refinement — iterate on proposal, secure tentative holds on accommodations and exclusive access
4. Booking & Confirmation — finalize bookings (private jets, riads, dining, tours), provide comprehensive itinerary doc
5. Pre-Trip Preparation — coordinate logistics, packing recommendations, special-request comms (dietary, security)
6. In-Trip Concierge — 24/7 support, real-time adjustments, flawless execution
7. Post-Trip Follow-up — feedback, update client profile, nurture relationship for future travel
