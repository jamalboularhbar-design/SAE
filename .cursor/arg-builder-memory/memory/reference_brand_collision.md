**The collision:**

In April 2026 at GITEX Africa, the Government of Morocco signed a **USD 1.28 billion MoU** with Nexus Core Systems to build the **Nexus AI Factory** — a sovereign AI infrastructure platform combining an HPC data center, a Center of Excellence, and an Innovation Hub. 500 MW planned capacity, powered by TAQA Morocco renewables, backed technologically by Nvidia and NAVER Cloud. The U.S. Ambassador to Morocco posted publicly endorsing the project as a U.S.–Morocco strategic milestone.

**Sources (used in the risk assessment):**
- TechAfrica News — Morocco Launches Nexus AI Factory (Apr 2026)
- Morocco World News — Nexus Factory at GITEX Africa
- Ecofin Agency — Morocco signs $1.28B MoU
- Intelligent CIO — Nexus Core Systems 500 MW factory backed by NAVER Cloud
- US Ambassador to Morocco (X / Twitter) — public endorsement

**Why this matters for ARG-Builder:**
- A "NexusAI" parent brand in Morocco competes with this project for trademark, search, and buyer mindshare for years.
- Asymmetric trademark exposure: Nexus Core Systems is likely to file (or has filed) in OMPIC classes covering AI services, data services, and consulting. A small lab using "NexusAI" in those classes faces a high legal cost asymmetry if challenged.
- Every "NexusAI" inbound search will land prospects on the $1.28B project first, not on the venture.
- The founder narrative (Marrakech, 25-year arc) compresses into noise next to a government-scale brand.

**Distinction that's safe:**
- **Nexus OS as a *product name*** is fine. It's narrower, refers to a specific runtime mounted at /os, and doesn't compete for the same SERP. Keep it.
- **Nexus** as the umbrella brand for the whole venture is the collision risk. Don't use NexusAI as the parent.

**Current resolution (2026-06-21):**
- Parent brand: **ARG-Builder**
- Sub-product: **Nexus OS** (kept)
- Domain: argbuilder.io
- nexusai.ma kept as legacy/redirect only

**How to apply:**
- If "NexusAI" appears in code or copy as the parent brand, that's a drift — replace with ARG-Builder.
- If Jamal asks about reviving the NexusAI parent (e.g., for the Morocco market), the right answer is "only after OMPIC + USPTO trademark clearance and confirmation Nexus Core Systems won't oppose." Do not assume that's been done.
- Nexus OS as a product within the ARG-Builder umbrella is the safe pattern. Don't merge them back.
